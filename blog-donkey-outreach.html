LIVESTOCK OUTREACH AT SONDU: EDUCATING DONKEY OWNERS

  • #Education
  • 23 August 2024

Livestock Market Outreach in Sondu Market aims to educate donkey owners and users on several key areas:

  • General donkey welfare, including nutrition, shelter, health care, feeding and drinking water, proper harnessing, tethering, and loading.
  • Raising awareness about donkeys being slaughtered for the skin trade in China. Farmers are encouraged to be vigilant and report any cases of lost, stolen, or missing donkeys to the relevant authorities.
  • Awareness of the general importance of donkeys.
