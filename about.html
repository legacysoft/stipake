About Us

Welcome to STIPA KENYA

Support for Tropical Initiatives in Poverty Alleviation (STIPA) aims at community empowerment for eradicating poverty, overcoming injustices and promoting social transformation among vulnerable rural and urban communities in Western Kenya.

  • The interventions cultivate social change through adoption of unique approaches and principles thereby modelling sustainable alternatives that strengthen the capacity of vulnerable community members to shift their priorities and expand their influence in leading change.
  • Partnership is a core principle of human rights based approach that we use to build power within, power to and power with local organizations and grassroot leadership critical to scaling our work and impact for sustainability.

Our advocacy is deeply rooted in our theory of change to inform and tap opportunities for investing towards collective transformational change with gender mainstreaming crosscutting.

Our History

From humble beginnings to becoming a force for good, we’ve been dedicated to making a difference.

Foundation of the NGO

1996

In 1996, a group of local professionals came together and identified the need to be registered as an organization to support local initiatives targeting the poor and marginalized communities. Their vision was to bridge a development gap they had recognized, specifically focusing on alleviating poverty in underserved areas.

Registration of the NGO

1997

Through their concerted efforts, the group successfully registered the organization as "Support for Tropical Initiatives in Poverty Alleviation." The NGO was officially registered on 25th September 1997 by the NGOs Coordination Board of Kenya, providing the platform needed to address the gap in development for the most vulnerable communities.

First Community Outreach

2016

Our first outreach program, serving over 500 individuals, marked a key milestone in our mission to support marginalized communities and make an immediate impact on their lives.

Key Partnership for Impact

2018

We partnered with local organizations and community leaders to amplify our outreach programs, creating lasting, positive change in rural and underserved areas.

Expanded Operations Nationwide

2020

Our programs grew to reach underserved communities across the country, providing education, healthcare, and economic development opportunities to the most vulnerable populations.

Reached 100,000+ Beneficiaries

2022

Through our outreach and empowerment programs, we impacted over 100,000 people, improving their livelihoods and providing hope for a better future.

Global Collaboration for Sustainable Development

2024

We have now joined forces with global partners to further our mission of sustainable development, addressing the root causes of poverty and promoting long-term solutions for marginalized communities.
